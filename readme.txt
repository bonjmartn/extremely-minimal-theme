FREE AND PREMIUM VERSIONS
------------------------------

This theme comes in two versions: Free and Premium.

Free Features

- Extremely minimal design
- Responsive layout
- Social media icons
- Detailed documentation

Premium Features

The Premium version has all of the free features, plus:

- Choose your own accent color
- 14 Google Fonts
- Choose your own color for headings and paragraph texts
- Open widget area on the top of the homepage
- Open widget area in the footer
- Premium, personal tech support
